What is sigma?

  • A sigma male is often portrayed as an individualist who prefers to operate outside of social hierarchies and traditional social norms, and who is not interested in conforming to societal expectations.
  • Some people use the term to describe a man who is self-sufficient and independent, and who is comfortable being alone or operating without the support of a group.
  • Some people use the term sigma male to describe a man who is highly intelligent and creative, and who is not constrained by conventional ways of thinking or problem-solving.
